# Mushroom Zucchini Lasagna (Lenten)

Meatless lasagna with an umami-bomb mushroom sauce that tastes like it has no business being vegetarian. Lent-friendly, low-carb (zucchini noodles), absolute showstopper.

## Sauce

### Ingredients
- 16 oz shiitake mushrooms, sliced
- 16 oz baby bella (cremini) mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 medium onion, diced small
- 2 stalks celery, diced small
- 1 carrot, diced small
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 4-6 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup red wine (Pinot Noir)
- 2 pints home garden tomato sauce (Lemon Boy or San Marzano)
- 1 tbsp heavy cream (to balance acidity/sweetness)
- Soy sauce to taste (umami boost)
- Salt and pepper

### Method
1. **Dry-sear mushrooms** in batches: hot pan, no oil, single layer. Let sit 3-4 min until deeply browned on one side. Flip, brown other side. Don't crowd. Don't wipe fond between batches. Set aside.
2. **Butter → soffritto:** Add butter to pan. Sauté diced onion, celery, carrot until soft (~8 min).
3. **Bloom paste:** Add tomato paste, cook 2-3 min until it darkens.
4. **Bloom herbs:** Add oregano and thyme, stir 30 sec.
5. **Garlic:** Add garlic, stir 1 min — don't burn.
6. **Deglaze:** Add wine and mushrooms. Scrape fond. Cook until wine smell is gone.
7. **Tomatoes:** Add garden tomato sauce. Stir in cream and soy sauce.
8. **Simmer:** Reduce 45 min minimum. Longer = better. (Next time: start sauce early, reduce longer.)

## Assembly

### Zucchini Noodles
- 3-4 large zucchini, sliced lengthwise ~¼" thick (mandoline ideal)
- Salt slices generously, lay on paper towels 20-30 min
- Pat dry thoroughly
- **NEXT TIME:** Roast at 400°F on sheet pans for 10 min to drive off more moisture

### Ricotta Layer
- 15 oz ricotta
- Fresh basil, chopped
- Salt and pepper
- (Mix together)

### Build
1. Thin layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish
2. Zucchini slices (single layer, slight overlap)
3. Ricotta mixture + a little shredded mozzarella
4. Repeat layers
5. Top with sauce and remaining mozzarella

### Bake
- 400°F covered for 45 min
- Uncover, bake 20 min more
- Rest 10 min before serving

## Notes
- **First cook was too wet** — zucchini released too much water despite salting/patting. Roasting the slices pre-assembly should fix this.
- **Longer sauce reduction** will also help — thicker sauce compensates for zucchini moisture.
- Heavy cream + soy sauce saved the sauce from being too sweet/acidic from the Lemon Boy tomatoes.
- Basil in both the ricotta AND the sauce was the right call.
- No dried porcini available — extra shiitake + baby bella worked great. Porcini would add another layer but isn't required.
- **Garden tomato plans:** 6 Super San Marzano + 4 regular San Marzano plants for summer 2026. 🍅
